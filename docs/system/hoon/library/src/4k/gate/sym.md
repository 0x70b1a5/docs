### `++sym`

Term

A term: a letter(lowercase), followed by letters, numbers, or `-`.

Source
------

    ++  sym
      %+  cook
        |=(a=tape (rap 3 ^-((list ,@) a)))
      ;~(plug low (star ;~(pose nud low hep)))
    ::

Examples
--------

    ~zod/try=> (scan "sam-2" sym)
    215.510.507.891
    ~zod/try=> `@t`(scan "sam-2" sym)
    'sam-2'
    ~zod/try=> (scan "sym" sym)
    7.174.515
