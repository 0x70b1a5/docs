### `++qual`

Mold of 4 type tuple

A `++qual` is a tuple of four of the types passed in.

Source
------

        ++  qual  |*  [a=$+(* *) b=$+(* *) c=$+(* *) d=$+(* *)] ::  just a quadruple

Examples
--------

    ~zod/try=> *(qual date time tape cord)
    [p=[[a=%.y y=0] m=0 t=[d=0 h=0 m=0 s=0 f=~]] q=~292277024401-.1.1 r="" s='']
